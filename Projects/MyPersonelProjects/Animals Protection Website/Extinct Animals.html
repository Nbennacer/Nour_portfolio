Some of the Extincted Animales

Thylacine

The last time that Thylacine was seen is on September 7th, 1936. The species was originally from Australia and it was declared extinct due to illegal hunting. Thylacine is also called "Tasmanian wolf" or "Tasmanian tiger".


Steller's Sea Cow

Steller's sea cow was a spicy that lived in an islands between Russia and Alaska in the Bering Sea. The spicy was extinct in 1768 only 27 years after its first discovery by human. Steller’s sea cow was a big animal and very slow it is why it was easy for hunters to catch it.


Dodo

Dodo bird is a spicy of bird that once lived in the island of Mauritius. The bird has extinct in the late of 17th century just after first human who settled island of Mauritius. Because Dodo was a weak bird that can not fly, it was an easy target to human’s domestic animals such as dogs to hunt. Also, humans were hunting the animal for its meat.
